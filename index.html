Fun Fact of the Day

Did you know? A giraffe's height can be 5.5 meters or 18 feet!

Giraffes are the tallest mammals on Earth, with their legs alone being taller than many humans at about 6 feet (1.8 meters). These magnificent creatures use their height to browse on leaves and buds in treetops that few other animals can reach.
